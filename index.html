Abstract

The MANTA-RAY system represents a breakthrough in soft surface manipulation technology, combining advanced robotics with intelligent control algorithms to achieve unprecedented performance in handling deformable materials. This research introduces novel methodologies for real-time surface adaptation and manipulation, demonstrating significant improvements over existing approaches. Our system integrates cutting-edge sensing technologies with adaptive control mechanisms to enable precise manipulation of soft surfaces across various applications.
